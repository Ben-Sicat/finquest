##  Saving for Needs: A Practical Approach

It's great you're focusing on needs!  Here's how to allocate your income effectively:

**1.  Prioritize Needs:**

* **50% of your income should be dedicated to needs.** This is the foundation of your financial stability. 
* **Needs are essentials for daily living.**  This includes:
    * **Housing:** Rent or mortgage payments
    * **Utilities:** Electricity, water, gas, internet
    * **Food:** Groceries, basic meals
    * **Transportation:** Public transport, fuel, car maintenance
